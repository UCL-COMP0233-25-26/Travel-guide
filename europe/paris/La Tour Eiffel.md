# La Tour Eiffel

We want to visit the Eiffel Tower because it is an iconic symbol of Paris, representing the perfect blend of romance and engineering marvel. We would love to ascend to the top for a panoramic view of Paris, experience the grandeur of this "Iron Lady", and witness its spectacular light show at night.

More info at [wikivoyage](https://en.wikivoyage.org/wiki/Eiffel_Tower) and [wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eiffel_Tower)
